Most SMBs don't have a risk register. They have a chat thread and a spreadsheet that nobody updates.

That works until a risk becomes an incident — and you spend the next week explaining to a client or insurer what you had documented. The answer is usually "not much."

A structured risk register isn't a compliance checkbox. It's the tool that tells you which operational, financial, and reputational risks need treatment, which ones you've accepted, and which ones have breached your risk appetite and need to escalate to a director or board. Axel gives you that register — and keeps it current.

WHAT'S INCLUDED

Everything a managed risk register needs.

Risk and opportunity register

Log every risk and opportunity with a title, description, category, owner, and treatment. The register gives you an audit trail — who changed what, and when.

  • Risk and opportunity types
  • Category pillars (Corporate, Build, Go-To-Market, Deliver)
  • Owner assignment and review dates
  • Full change history

Likelihood × consequence scoring

Risks are scored on a 5×5 matrix — likelihood against consequence. The residual score (post-treatment) drives the severity band: Low, Medium, High, or Critical.

  • Inherent and residual risk scores
  • Appetite threshold per category
  • Outside-appetite flagging
  • Trend tracking (improving, stable, deteriorating)
Critical (15–25) High (10–14) Medium (5–9) Low (1–4)

Escalation workflows

When a risk score breaches your defined appetite, it escalates automatically. Owners get notified. Directors can review and accept or return for re-treatment. Nothing sits unacknowledged.

  • Automatic escalation on appetite breach
  • Director and board-level review queues
  • Closure request process
  • Monthly review reminders

Board-ready reporting

Monthly reports are generated automatically — risk heatmap, status summary, top risks by residual score, and any risks outside appetite. PDF or in-app, ready to table at your next meeting.

  • Executive summary dashboard
  • By-pillar and by-status breakdowns
  • Top risks by current residual
  • Month-on-month trend

THE PROCESS

From setup to first board report in a week.

  1. 1

    Set your risk appetite

    Define what score is acceptable for each category of risk in your business. High appetite for strategic risk, low appetite for safety or regulatory risk — you set the thresholds. Axel flags anything that breaches them.

  2. 2

    Populate your register

    Log your known risks — or we run a facilitated risk identification session and do it with you. Assign owners, score each risk, and set a treatment plan. Most registers are live within two days of kickoff.

  3. 3

    Review on your cycle

    Owners get a reminder when a review is due. They update the score, treatment status, and trend direction. The dashboard updates immediately. Nothing relies on a manual report being run.

  4. 4

    Table it at your monthly meeting

    The monthly report is generated automatically at the end of each period — a heatmap, top risks, status summary, and any escalations outstanding. Ready to PDF and distribute, or present from the dashboard directly.
