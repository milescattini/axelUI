WHAT THIS GETS YOU

A certificate that enterprise and government procurement recognise.

ISO 9001 is often a prerequisite for government panels, enterprise supply chain registration, and many industry-specific contracts. Having it on file means you don't get screened out before the work even starts.

Axel builds the quality management system that certification bodies audit against, and stays involved through the audit itself. You don't need to understand the standard in detail — that's our job.

THE PROCESS

From kickoff to certified.

  1. 1

    Scoping call

    We confirm your certification scope — which products, services, or locations are included — and identify any industry-specific requirements. We'll also help you choose an accredited certification body if you don't have one.

  2. 2

    QMS build and documentation

    We build your quality management system into SharePoint and write every required document. Typical time from kickoff to audit-ready is two to four weeks for most businesses.

  3. 3

    Stage 1 audit — document review

    The certification body reviews your documentation. We sit alongside you, handle any questions about how the system works, and address any findings before Stage 2.

  4. 4

    Stage 2 audit — implementation audit

    The auditor verifies that your QMS is live and working. We're in the room for the full audit, respond to nonconformances on the day, and stay available until your certificate is issued.
