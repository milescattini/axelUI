THE PROCESS

Set up in a day. Useful from week one.

  1. 1

    Connect your accounts

    Authorise Xero and ServiceM8 via OAuth — read-only access, no credentials stored. Existing invoices and jobs import automatically. Most businesses are connected inside an hour.

  2. 2

    Load your pipeline

    Add your open deals — or import from a spreadsheet. Set a stage, a value, a probability, and a target close date. The forecast line populates immediately.

  3. 3

    Watch actuals reconcile

    As invoices land in Xero, they appear as actuals against the relevant deal. Forecast vs actual variance is visible on the same chart — no export, no pivot table.

  4. 4

    Review and adjust monthly

    Use the monthly variance report to see where your forecast drifted from reality. Adjust stage probabilities, close dates, or deal values. The forecast updates on save.
